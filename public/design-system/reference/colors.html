Usage rules
The short list.
Do
One red per screen

Primary CTA, data accent, or R-mark — pick one. Red loses power when repeated.

Do
Black for depth

Full black (#000) and near-black (#171717) are the default heavy voice — not a dark grey.

Don't
Mix sport accents

Golf green and Baseball navy together reads as a generic dashboard. Pick the product context.

Don't
Gradient the brand

Rapsodo Red stays flat. Depth comes from black, shadow, and photography.
