Clear space

Don't
The wordmark is a system. Break the system and it stops meaning Rapsodo.
Don't stretch or squish.
Don't place on off-brand gradients.
Don't rotate. The italic is the angle.
Don't drop-shadow or bevel.

Voice
How Rapsodo talks in copy.

Do

  • Write like a coach — direct, confident, specific.
  • Lead with the stat. "312 yd carry" before "great drive."
  • Name the player and the moment. "Dialed-in on the 7-iron."
  • Short sentences. Short paragraphs. Momentum.

Don't

  • Don't be breathy or motivational. No "unleash your potential."
  • Don't use exclamation marks for emphasis.
  • Don't hide behind jargon — ball-flight physics stays in the help doc, not the CTA.
  • Don't use emoji in brand voice.
